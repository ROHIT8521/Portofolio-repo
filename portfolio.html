Intelligent Helmet for Miners

A smart helmet for Miners enables the condition of Miners mining in the underground to be relayed to the supervision unit by means of various sensors embedded on the helmet, so that appropriate safety measures can be taken accordingly.

Automatic Room Lights using Arduino and PIR sensor

In this Project, the lights in the room will automatically turn ON upon detecting a Human motion and stay turned ON until the person has left. The main elements of the setup are Arduino, PIR sensor and Relay module.

Smart Parking system(Design Thinking)

It is a protocol for car parking system that is completely unmanned and safe from any kind of theft from car in parking. It used the concept of Microcontroller such as Arduino UNO and encoder, L293D motor driving IC. It was presented in Design Thinking programme.
